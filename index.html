About Us

What is Cannacoin?

Cannacoin($CCN) is an alternative, instant payment system for the cannabis industry. It was first deployed on March 28, 2014 as a solution to the "cash only" predicament set on growers, dispensaries, and consumers alike through Federal regulation as well as a response to the Global Financial Crisis of 2008. It was the brainchild of the pseudonymous Subtoshi, the founder of the popular, but now defunct, "NWGT.org" (Northwest Green Thumb) online medical cannabis forum based in the Pacific Northwest. Cannacoin Prime picks up where Cannacoin Classic left off. It is a smart contract based token and is intended for use within the independent, medical, and recreational cannabis communities as a medium of exchange, a unit of account, and a store of value for cannabis and cannabis related products and services, worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

got questions?

  • Who created Cannacoin?

    Cannacoin was originally deployed on March 28, 2014 by the creators of the mega popular online medical cannabis forum called Northwest Green Thumb located in the Pacific Northwest. It was meant to assist the members of the forum but many were afraid and it caused an uproar. The brothers eventually moved on to other projects but a few members of the original development team kept the project alive. The original Cannacoin is now called Cannacoin Classic.

  • What is Cannacoin Classic?

    Cannacoin Classic is the original coin developed by Subtoshi and JamesonWA that was deployed on Northwest Green Thumb in 2014. It was an open source project but lost support over the years so the remaining development team decided to release a new smart contract based token to pick up where Cannacoin Classic left off. The new token is called Cannacoin Prime.

  • Why should I use Cannacoin?

    Let's face it, handling all that cash is an extremely risky effort plus banks are a hassle for cannabis businesses. The world is changing. The idea behind our project is that, simply put, Bitcoin is becoming too valuable to spend on everyday purchases like cannabis and that cryptocurrency, in one form or another, will eventually be ubiquitous.

  • How can I acquire Cannacoin Prime?

    We are giving new members of our Discord a small amount of Cannacoin Prime for joining up and will be airdropping larger amounts to members over the next few months. Soon there will be a faucet and tipbot on Discord as well. Ultimately, we plan to deploy on Trader Joe's DEX. Please join our Discord and share your MetaMask wallet address to acquire your Airdrops. If you are an existing holder of Cannacoin Classic, you will have the opportunity to swap your coins for the new token on a 1:1 basis until January 1st, 2023. We will be releasing a web based wallet that will facilitate the migration. Stay tuned.

  • What is the max supply of Cannacoin Prime?

    The max supply of Cannacoin Prime is 13,141,591. It is a prime number based on Pi. It is approximately the same max supply that Cannacoin Classic was for economic parity.

  • What blockchain is Cannacoin Prime built on?

    Cannacoin Prime is proud to be built on the Avalanche blockchain. It is an ultra-high speed/low-fee blockchain. Transactions times are usually under 10 seconds and gas fees are usually around a nickel. It is an efficient and seamless experience.

  • What is the contract address for Cannacoin Prime?

    Cannacoin Prime can be found on Snowtrace.io at "https://snowtrace.io/token/0x485be08f2563862dee92e2e3c252f08f8a75a776" and you can easily find the contract address listed there along with other pertinent information about the token as well as a complete transaction history.

  • Does Cannacoin Prime have a wallet?

    As of now, MetaMask is the preferred wallet for Cannacoin Prime. In the future we will be rolling out a proprietary socially enabled wallet so you can add and chat with your friends as well as exchange Cannacoin and more.

  • Is Cannacoin hiring?

    Yes. We are always on the lookout for new talent and support. Please contact us and let us know a little bit about your background and what cannabis and cryptocurrency mean to you. We look forward to hearing from you soon.
